About Me

Thanks for checking out the site! I was photojournalist for a long time and have been assigned some really cool stories over the years. Check out the Videos tab.

For two years I taught high school Graphic Design and Audio/Video Production. It was fun and challenging teaching others to not only understand how to complete an assignment, but give them the tools to create projects for themselves.

Recently I've been getting into Web Development and have been focused on learning HTML, CSS, Javascript and other technologies that let me create websites.

My focus is to keep learning, growing, and creating content through videos, designs, and websites.
